How does ThriftIQ work?

Snap a photo of an item (or scan its barcode), and ThriftIQ identifies it and estimates its resale value on eBay and Facebook Marketplace, along with selling fees, shipping, and how quickly it tends to sell. Enter the store's asking price to get an instant Buy, Maybe, or Skip verdict with your estimated profit.

Are the estimated values guaranteed?

No. The values are AI-generated estimates meant to help you make a decision. Actual sale prices vary with condition, timing, and your local market.

How many free scans do I get?

You get 15 free scans every 2 months. ThriftIQ Pro unlocks unlimited scans, barcode scanning, and the full profit & inventory tools.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are handled by Apple. On your iPhone, open the Settings app, tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions to change or cancel ThriftIQ Pro anytime.

How do I delete my account and data?

In the app, go to Settings → Delete Account & Data. This permanently erases your account and scan history from your device.
