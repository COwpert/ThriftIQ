ThriftIQ Privacy Policy

Effective June 15, 2026

ThriftIQ ("we", "the app") helps you photograph an item and estimate its resale value. We built ThriftIQ to keep your data on your device as much as possible. This policy explains what we collect, why, and who it's shared with.

What we collect

  • Account info. When you create an account we store your name and email. If you choose Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google, we receive a user identifier and (if you allow it) your name and email from that provider. Email-and-password accounts are kept on your device only, with the password stored as a salted hash — never in plain text.
  • Photos you scan. When you scan an item, that photo is sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to identify the item and estimate its value. The photo is used only to generate that result and is not stored by us afterward.
  • Scan history & inventory. The items you scan, the prices you enter, and your sold/profit records are stored locally on your device. We do not upload this to a server.
  • Purchases. ThriftIQ Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple. We never see or store your payment-card details.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your personal information.
  • We don't show third-party ads.
  • We don't use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

Who we share data with

We share data only with the service providers needed to run the app:

  • Anthropic, PBC — processes the photo you scan to identify the item. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple and subscription billing. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Google LLC — only if you choose Sign in with Google. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Data storage & security

Your account credentials are stored in the device Keychain. Your scan history and inventory are stored locally on your device. All network requests use encrypted HTTPS connections.

Deleting your data

You can delete your account and all of its scan history at any time from Settings → Delete Account & Data inside the app. Because this data lives on your device, deleting it removes it.

Children

ThriftIQ is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
